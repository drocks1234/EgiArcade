Minesweeper Game

Oh no, you clicked a bomb!

How to Play:

  • Left-click on a cell to reveal it.
  • Right-click (or long-press on mobile) on a cell to flag it as a potential mine.
  • A number on a revealed cell indicates the number of neighboring mines.
  • Avoid clicking on a cell with a mine; it ends the game.
  • Clear all non-mine cells to win the game.

Strategy Tip:

Start by revealing cells in areas with fewer neighboring mines. Use the numbers on revealed cells to deduce the locations of mines.
